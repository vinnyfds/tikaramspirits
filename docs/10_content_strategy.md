# Content Strategy & Copywriting Matrix

**Brand Voice:** Poetic, Craft-Forward, Sun-Soaked, Timeless.
**Rule:** Avoid generic marketing fluff ("Best in class"). Use sensory words ("Oak," "Salt," "Heat," "Time").

---

## 1. Global Microcopy (System Text)
*These appear on every page.*

* **Navbar:**
    * Links: "The Spirits", "Our Story", "Cocktails", "Events", "Visit Us".
    * CTA Button: "Find a Store".
* **Footer:**
    * Headline: "Join Team Tikaram."
    * Subhead: "Unlock exclusive access to limited releases, events, and a welcome gift on your first visit."
    * Input Placeholder: "Enter your email address..."
    * Button: "Sign Up".
    * Copyright: "© 2025 Tikaram Spirits, Florida. Please Drink Responsibly."
* **Age Gate (Modal):**
    * Headline: "Are you of legal drinking age?"
    * Subhead: "To enter this site, you must be 21 years or older."
    * Button: "Enter Site".
    * Error: "You must be of legal drinking age to visit Tikaram Spirits."

## 4. Product Detail Template (`/spirits/[slug]`)
*Matches `@07_layout_specs.md` - Section 3. Use this pattern for ALL products.*

### Example: Ponce de Leon Rum
* **Header Title:** "Ponce de Leon Rum"
* **Header Sub:** "Aged 12 Years • 40% ABV"
* **Romance Copy (The Story):**
    "Named after the explorer who sought the Fountain of Youth, this rum might be the closest thing to it. Aged for 12 long years in American Oak, it absorbs the humid Florida air to create a profile that is deep, complex, and impossibly smooth."
* **Tasting Notes (The Wheel):**
    * *Nose:* "Toasted coconut, vanilla bean, leather."
    * *Palate:* "Dried apricot, dark chocolate, warm spice."
    * *Finish:* "Long, warm, and slightly smoky."
* **Serving Suggestion:** "Best enjoyed neat, or with a single large cube of ice."
