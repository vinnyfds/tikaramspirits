# Project Brief: Tikaram Spirits

**Project Name:** Tikaram Spirits Brand Showcase
**Domain:** tikaramspirits.com
**Primary Goal:** Brand Awareness & Retail Conversion ("Find a Store").

## Executive Summary
Tikaram Spirits is a luxury, hand-crafted alcohol brand featuring a diverse portfolio: Rum, Bourbon, Paan Liqueur, and Tequila. The website acts as a premium digital showroom that immerses users in the "Sun-Soaked, Craft-Forward" lifestyle of Florida, ultimately driving them to physical retail locations to purchase.

## Success Metrics
1.  **Store Locator Usage:** % of visitors who search for a location.
2.  **Lead Generation:** Number of users signing up for the "First Bottle Discount."
3.  **Engagement:** Time on page for "Our Story" and Product Detail pages.

## Key Features
1.  **Age Verification Gate:** Strict 21+ entry modal (Cookie-based persistence).
2.  **Immersive Product Pages:** High-fidelity layouts for 5 core products.
3.  **Store Locator:** Map interface to find retailers.
4.  **Discount Coupon System:** Email signup -> Verification -> Digital Coupon delivery.
5.  **Events Calendar:** Showcase tasting events.

## Target Audience
Connoisseurs of fine spirits, mixology enthusiasts, and luxury lifestyle consumers looking for unique, heritage-rich beverages.
